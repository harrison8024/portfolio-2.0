About Me.

Hi there! Welcome to my page. I’m Harrison B. Chen, a Taiwanese-American, and definitely a boba tea lover! I was originally from Taiwan and moved to the United States in 2006. I have always been fascinated by science and technology, which leads me to obtaining a bachelor degree in Physics. I have completed two researches (Gamma-ray Bursts and Molecular Symmetry) and published my first paper in a physics journal, MDPI. After graduating college, my friend and I started a startup call InternLogic, which is a platform that helps college students find internships based on their Myers’ Briggs personality traits. I was working in business development and sales for our company at the time, but soon I became immensely interested in applications development. I then begin to oversee quality assurance of our website. This is when I started taking coding classes, and found my passion in programming. I love problem solving, enhancing my skills, and absorbing new information. Here I am today pursuing my dream as a web and software developer.
